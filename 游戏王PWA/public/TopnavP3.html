Basic rules:
1. Card group: main card group and sub-card group. Minimum 40 CARDS for master card group, maximum 60 CARDS; 15 sub-cards. The primary card group is used as a match; The secondary card group is the two games between if the main card group is not strong enough against the opponent, can be with the main card group of some CARDS to do a one-to-one exchange, in order to achieve the best against different types of opponents.
2. Restricted CARDS, quasi-restricted CARDS and prohibited CARDS: the combination of card groups should follow these rules. The restriction is that each card group (including sub-card group) can only put 1 card; The quasi - limit card is only 2 CARDS per group; A prohibited card is one that cannot be used in an official OCG tournament. In addition, not in this range of CARDS, the same kind of CARDS in the card group can not be more than 3.
3. Winning and losing conditions: the other party's LP has been deducted (each party has 8000LP); Must draw the card and no card can draw the case of negative; For special effects (e.g. exodias, countdown to the end, ouija, etc.); A draw in which both sides meet the conditions for victory.
4. Battlefield introduction: the monster area has 5 grids, which are used for placing monster CARDS, attack monster CARDS are placed vertically, and defense monster CARDS are placed horizontally. The magic trap area has 5 squares, which must be displayed side by side when the spell is active. Also on the back of the display in this area, such as the corresponding time to start.

Playing methods and winning conditions

Start step: the turn player declares to be "in combat".

Combat steps: choose one of the monsters on your field to attack only one of the opponent's monsters. You can attack your opponent directly when there are no monsters on the field. Enter the harm step after the declaration. The damage step ends and the combat step continues, and so on. But basically, a monster can only attack once.

Damage steps: the steps to calculate the damage and determine the outcome of the battle, refer to the "monster fighting rules". The damage step ends and returns to the combat step.

End step: the combat and damage steps are repeated until there are no more monsters to attack. Then the battle phase ends.
